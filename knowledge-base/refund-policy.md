## Damage & defect claims
- For claims of a damaged or defective item, ask the customer for a photo of
  the item (and packaging) before issuing the refund. Frame the request as
  helping us find the cause and prevent recurrence — not as proof of honesty.
- Low-value claims (under the evidence threshold) may be auto-resolved without a
  photo, since the friction outweighs the risk.
- Once a photo is provided, resolve the refund up to the eligible maximum.
