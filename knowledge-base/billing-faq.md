# Billing FAQ

Grounding source for billing and payment questions.

## Shipping charges
- Orders **over $50 ship free**. Orders at or below $50 carry a flat shipping
  fee shown at checkout. If a customer asks why they were charged shipping,
  check the order total against the $50 threshold and explain.

## Refund timing
- Approved refunds take **5–10 business days** to appear, depending on the
  customer's bank. The refund posts to the original payment method.

## Duplicate / double charges
- We **never** auto-reverse a charge. A duplicate-charge report must be
  **escalated** to the billing team.
- Common benign cause: a temporary **authorization hold** that looks like a
  second charge and drops off in **3–5 business days**. Mention this, but still
  escalate so a human can confirm and reverse if needed.

## Unauthorized charges
- Any charge the customer says they **did not authorize** is a potential fraud /
  account-security issue and must be **escalated immediately**. The agent must
  not dismiss it or auto-resolve.

## Payment methods & invoices
- We accept major credit/debit cards. Invoices/receipts are emailed at purchase
  and can be re-sent on request (a routine, auto-resolvable task).
