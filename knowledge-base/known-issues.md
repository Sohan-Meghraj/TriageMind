# Known Issues

Current operational issues the agent can reference so it doesn't over-promise or
escalate things that are already understood.

## Carrier delays
- Some regions are seeing **carrier delays of 2–3 extra business days**. If a
  delivery is only a few days late and the customer is calm, reassure them with
  this context — it's auto-resolvable, no escalation needed.

## Tracking not updating
- Tracking that hasn't updated for **24–48 hours** is usually normal; the parcel
  is moving even if scans are sparse. Reassure and set expectations.

## Delivered-but-not-received
- If tracking shows **delivered** but the customer says it never arrived, this
  needs an investigation and possibly a carrier claim. **Escalate** — do not
  auto-refund or auto-resolve.

## Known defect batch
- A limited batch of the travel kettle (SKU TK-200) shipped with a faulty power
  switch. For confirmed TK-200 defect reports, a photo still helps us track the
  batch, but the claim is pre-validated — handle per the refund policy.

## Outages
- No active platform outages. If a customer reports being unable to check out or
  log in and nothing here covers it, escalate so a human can confirm.
